Chapter 10

Looking at the club outside the car window, Amelia spoke to Carlee with a look of disbelief. "Why did you bring me to a club?"

"There's only one reason people go to clubs-to forget their troubles for a while and have some fun."Dragging Amelia out of the car, Carlee swept them through the entrance. "You really have rotten luck, you know. Bad day after bad day. But luckily, you still have me, and I plan to make you very happy tonight."

Amelia could not deny that life with Jaxton would turn into pure chaos.

But when she thought about Wyatt, Amelia believed he would never add to her problems.

That thought flickered through her mind just as Carlee swept her into a private room.

Determined to lift her friend's spirits, Carlee called over a handful of striking men from the club to join them.

Soon,Amelia found herself at the center of attention, encircled by charming faces. Carlee seized the microphone and announced, "Gentlemen, whoever brings a smile to my friend's face tonight is in for a generous treat!"

Everyone at the club knew Carlee never hesitated to spend big.

The reward she dangled wasn't small change; it was at least one hundred thousand dollars.

Her offer sent the men flocking to Amelia's side.

"You've spent two years as Jaxton's fianc√©e, and you've suffered a lot because of that.Since you're ending your engagement, you can finally relax and have fun now." Carlee leaned in and handed a glass of wine into Amelia's hand. "I snuck this wine out from my dad's private stash only for you. Enjoy it."

Grateful for Carlee's good intentions and still carrying a weight of frustration because of Jaxton, Amelia accepted the wine.

She forgot,however, that it had been ages since she last drank, and before long, a dizzy spell washed over her.

Carlee noticed Amelia's dazed expression and quickly ushered the men away. "You all right,Amelia?"

"My head's spinning a bit."

"Feeling dizzy? Don't worry; I'll get you home right away."

With one arm around Amelia, Carlee led her out of the room, only to see her friend's eyes flutter shut.Amelia was clearly drunk.

Seeing Amelia in this state, Carlee began to regret her decision to bring Amelia here today.

Considering the recent scandal involving the Morrison Group, Carlee feared Jaxton might give Amelia even more trouble if he found out about this. She quickly decided to slip her out the back.

At the rear entrance, the elevator was usually off-limits. This time, as Carlee approached with Amelia, it slid open.

Carlee believed fortune seemed to be smiling on her this time.

Just then, Wyatt stepped out of the elevator, the heavy scent of alcohol hitting his senses. A frown appeared on his face, and distaste flashed in his eyes.

Davin rushed forward and planted himself as a barrier before Wyatt.

Leaning heavily against Carlee, Amelia let her hair drape over her face, so Davin didn't recognize her.

The moment Carlee made her way to the elevator with Amelia, Davin said, "Excuse me, that elevator isn't open to everyone.Please choose another one."

With her attention fixed on Amelia, Carlee hadn't even noticed anyone approach until she found her path blocked.Nearly losing her balance with Amelia in tow, she felt her temper flare. "Whether it's private or not,this is still a club. You think you own the elevator?"

Without another word, she pressed forward, determined to get inside.

Watching her push past, Davin furrowed his brows. Wyatt alone used this private elevator, not just for the club but for the entire building.

Additional protests formed on his lips, but Wyatt interjected, his voice already tight with impatience, "Get security here.Now."

Realizing that Wyatt's patience was wearing thin, Davin quickly called the security team.

It didn't take long before the security team arrived.

Carlee spotted a few familiar faces in the security team since she had been here often, but she was shocked by the captain's sudden respectful posture toward Davin.

"Mr. Kirk, how can I help you? Oh, Mr.Stewart..."

The captain at first assumed Davin was here on his own. However, when he caught sight of Wyatt, his composure shifted in a heartbeat.

He barely managed a greeting before Davin interjected, "Make these two leave. And make sure nobody gets near this elevator from now on."

Hearing the order, the captain barked at his team, "Why are you all still standing there? Get them to leave.Now."

Being manhandled by staff was a slap in the face for Carlee, who was used to getting her way. As soon as one guard reached for Amelia, Carlee snapped, swatting his hand aside and yelling,"Try it,and you'll regret it!"

That threat made the security crew pause and glance at their boss. With no response from him, they grew even rougher.

Things escalated quickly, and Amelia stumbled, nearly crashing to the floor.

Carlee was furious and anxious, almost in tears, when a clear, cool voice said, "Stop."

Instantly, every guard froze in place. As Carlee's confusion mounted, she suddenly felt Amelia pulled from her side. Wyatt had swept Amelia up, cradling her in his arms with her flushed cheeks catching the ligh Wyatt's gaze turned deep as he took in the sight of Amelia like this.

The scene terrified Carlee, and she quickly approached Wyatt.

Bathed in the bar's lights, Amelia's beauty could silence a crowd. Even Carlee, who'd seen her countless times, felt enchanted.

So,Carlee's first thought was that Wyatt had taken a liking to Amelia.

That realization landed on her like a blow. What kind of mess had she stirred up now?

Anxious, Carlee rolled up her sleeves and exclaimed, "Keep your hands off her!"
