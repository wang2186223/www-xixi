Chapter 87

After she left, someone quietly stepped out from the shadows.

Jessa asked, "Is it really worth helping her like this?"

"No choice, I can't compete with the other two in terms of talent, so I have to resort to some tricks!"

Andy looked downcast, disappointment clear in her eyes. "I thought if I helped her deal with Ella, she'd help me get first place. Didn't expect it to turn out like this."

Jessa's face twisted in frustration. "Why don't we get some dirt on her?Record everything she says to keep her from turning on us!"

"You're right, that's what we should do!"

Andy nodded in agreement.

The two of them whispered and plotted for a long time.

Meanwhile, Ava listened to the recording over and over again with her headphones on.

She was so mad she gritted her teeth, wishing she could rush over and tear Worry-free's face apart.

Thinking it over, she realized that despite knowing her for a long time, she had never seen her true face.

'What does she even look like?'

Without realizing it, Ava wandered into the garden. Seeing the RV not far away, her eyes lit up and she was about to run over when Arthur's assistant, Efren Britton, suddenly appeared and stopped her.

"Ms. Davis! Please leave here right now!"

"Why?"

Ava was unconvinced and was about to push past him when she suddenly sensed something was off.

The car was parked there perfectly fine, and there was no one around,so why was it shaking?

The special glass of this car allowed those inside to see out, while those outside couldn't see in.

As the car shook more and more violently, she suddenly thought of something and grabbed Efren's arm. "Is Arthur in there?"

Her eyes seemed to burn as she stared at him, trying to get an answer from his face.

Efren shook his head. "I have nothing to say about this matter! Please leave immediately!"

Ava's expression suddenly changed, and she wished she could rush over.

But besides Efren, there were several bodyguards around.

She smiled bitterly. "I understand, and don't tell anyone I was here!"

"Gotit!"

Not far away on the balcony, Lily saw everything and sneered,nudging Alan. Doesn't it look like a jealous fight?"

Ella's identity had already been exposed, so there was nothing to hide.

At this moment, Lily and Alan were standing together, joking.

Alan lit a cigarette and took a deep drag. "I think it's pretty funny!"

"What's so funny? I need to go teach Ava a lesson!"

With that, Lily, wearing high heels, ran downstairs.

Lily mocked, "Ava? You look pale-who upset you?"

"Stay away from me, I don't want to talk to you right now!"

"Take it easy. I wanted to take you for a walk in the garden. I just saw Ms.Worry-Free get into the RV. How about we go check it out?"

Ava glared at her angrily. "I know what you mean, you just want to anger me. No way!"

She glared at Lily. "Your name is Lily,right? I'll remember you! I'll make sure you disappear from this industry completely!"

Watching Ava walk away, her hair seemed to be bristling with anger,and Lily couldn't help but laugh.

Alan was speechless.

'It's just women and deceitful people who are hard to deal with. The two of them were too scheming.

He stood on the balcony, able to see everything happening around him.Just as he was wondering whether to inform Arthur, the RV door opened.

As their gazes met. Alan was so scared that he ran away.

"Tll head back first. you come later."

Her voice was hoarse from all the shouting, and Ella spoke weakly.

She shot Arthur an angry glare.

Her watery eyes were full of charm, and her glare looked more like a silent invitation.

Arthur lightly licked his lower lip and smiled seductively. "Can't bear to leave me?"

"Stay away from me!"

Ella straightened her clothes, saw no one around, and hurriedly ran off.

Arthur stood there, tugging at his tie. "Liar!"

'Her insistence on divorce was clearly a tactic to attract me.'

Thinking about their recent climax, Arthur was satisfied, his eyes filled with desire.

The phone rang.

He glanced at it, his expression changing. "I'll be back at the company right away!"

The night grew darker.

Ella sat at her computer desk, her head aching. "What should I do? The other groups already have some ideas, but we still have nothing."

"Alright, haste makes waste, don't worry!"

Lily brought over a magazine. "These are works from the parent-child fashion week two years ago. Take a look and get some inspiration!"

"Okay!"

As time passed, Ella's eyes lit up."I can pretend to design parent-child outfits for dogs. That way, I'll feel better."

Lily was speechless.

She really has a rich imagination, but it indeed was a good idea.

With a new approach, things went much smoother.

Time flew by.

At dawn, Ella yawned, looking at the design on the computer,and smiled with satisfaction.

She checked the time; it was already 4 AM.

Lily was sleeping soundly on the bed, not having returned to her room last night.

Arthur had repeatedly mentioned he would come over at night, yet he didn't show up.

'Could he be with Ava?'

Thinking of this, Ella lowered her eyes, hid her complex emotions, and quickly fell asleep.

Early in the morning, Ella and Lily came to the living room as usual.

After breakfast, they learned that the program had been adjusted.

The live broadcast was paused, and the next one would be a runway show to display their designs, which meant they had to complete three outfits in two days.

With tight schedules and heavy tasks, everyone stopped chatting and rushed back to their studios to start working.

In the studio, Arthur didn't come, and neither did Ava.

Only Ella and Lily were exceptionally busy.

Time flew by, and two days passed quietly.

Early in the morning, everyone gathered in the living room.

"Hey everyone, the runway show is at 1 PM this afternoon. Please hurry and get your works ready. If you need anything,just let us know."

Victory or defeat hinged on this moment.

After this runway show, there would be an elimination round,and everyone wanted to perform well.

Ella was no exception. After the director finished speaking,everyone dispersed. Back in the studio, Ella took out the clothes.

"The child actors will come at noon, so please try on these clothes first!"

As a model in the same group, Ava glanced at the clothes and said, "T'll do my makeup first,there's still time to try them on later!"

Arthur responded indifferently, "I'll try them on first!"

He grabbed the clothes from Ella's hands and walked to the changing room. Watching his back,Ava frowned,feeling something was off.

Lily sneered. "What are you looking at? It's not yours.so don't stare.Be careful,or I'll gouge out your eyes!"
