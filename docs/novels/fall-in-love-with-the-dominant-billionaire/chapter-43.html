Well, since he had given it to her, how she handled it was her own business.

"Are you going to massage the legs again today?"

"Yes, I'm going to soak them in an herbal bath and get a massage."

She had specifically consulted with a senior physician and found that Malcolm's legs could be treated. It was just a matter of finding the right method. At least he could already feel somesensation, which proved that it was effective.

The doctors there were also paying close attention to Malcolm's condition, as there weren't many cases of paralyzed individuals being cured.

Malcolm raised an eyebrow with a hint of interest in his eyes.

"Did you add any new treatments?"

Reese had just taken out the items when she heard his tone and couldn't help but find it amusing.

"Why do I feel like you're enjoying this?"

The first time Reese saw Malcolm; his room was pitch black, without a hint of light. It was enough to see that he had lost interest in life. But now, even though his legs hadn't fully recovered, his spirit had already made a significant comeback.

Malcolm's lips curved into an enticing smile.Reese had encountered many men, but there were few like him who were exceptional.

With just one smile, Reese felt her heart racing.

But his next words sent shivers down her spine..

He said, "It's all thanks to my wife taking good care of me."

Although these words sounded warm,Reese wasn't used to it.

She raised her hand and said, "Enough. We're only putting on a show of affection outside. There's no need to pretend here."

"But you can't deny that you are my wife now,"he stubbornly declared.

Seeing his domineering expression, Reese felt like she had no way of dealing with him.

That's fine, just ignore it.

After giving Malcolm an injection and helping him prepare the bath, Reese was sweating on her forehead by the time she finished all the tasks. Under the light,her skin looked crystal clear, displaying her original complexion.

This was her natural skin color.

Reese was about to stand up, but suddenly, Malcolm grabbed her wrist.

She looked at him curiously and accidentally fell into his deep gaze.

"What...What's wrong with you?"

"Honey, are you hiding something from me? Like, do you still have some other skills?"

Malcolm's words carried profound meaning,especially with his eyes fixed attentively on Reese.

She didn't plan to let even the slightest expression on her face go unnoticed. Reese's heart was pounding and she felt a bit flustered, but facing Malcolm's inquisitive gaze,she was afraid that she might lose her composure and reveal her emotions. Most likely, he was astonished by her dazzling appearance today, realizing that she didn't resemble a typical Backwoods girl. In addition,she had exposed some of her skills earlier, so it would be strange if Malcolm didn't suspect anything.

They were standing quite close to each other, and with the steam filling the bathroom,Reese could see the smooth movement of Malcolm's Adam's apple, creating an alluring line. Along with the scorching heat emanating from him, it enveloped her completely.

Reese's heart was racing, almost leaping out of her throat. A blush rose on her cheeks, making her appear even more charming and adorable.

Malcolm slowly approached her, his lips touching her ear as he spoke in a low and husky voice, "Can you kiss?" Reese quickly snapped back to reality,pushing Malcolm away in a fluster, and she stood up as well."Why would I have anything to do with you?"

Surprisingly, she felt shy.

Malcolm became even more interested and said, "If you don't know how, I can teach you,honey."

Reese had always been stubborn, a person who refused to give in. Seeing Malcolm's disdainful expression, she naturally couldn't back down. She suppressed the turmoil in her heart, lifted her face with clear and determined eyes.

"What's the big deal about a kiss? I can do it. Besides,I won't kiss you, I won't fall for that."

Reese opened the bathroom door, touching her rapidly beating chest. Finally, she let out a long breath and said to Malcolm inside. "Come out quickly. I'm tired."

She still had to give him a massage.

"You better dress me first," Malcolm reminded her with a hearty laugh.

Reese slapped her forehead, realizing her mind had been completely muddled by him. She had forgotten that his legs weren't healed yet, and he couldn't stand up.

But she also didn't want to go back in.

"I will go find two servants."

Malcolm sat in the bathtub, coughed lightly while trying to hold back his laughter, and replied with an affirming sound. When the servants supported Malcolm out,he suddenly felt a sensation in his legs. Although standing was still difficult, he was extremely thrilled.

If Reese had really healed him, he would be able to stand up and no longer rely on a wheelchair for the rest of his life.

The servants didn't notice the change on Malcolm's face, and they dared not look at him carelessly. After all,Master's presence was intimidating, and just supporting him alone was enough to instill fear in them. They were afraid of making mistakes and angering him.

After lying down, Reese approached with a natural expression and clearer eyes. She sat cross-legged on the edge of the bed, grasping his thigh with both hands and gradually exerting force. Atfirst,Malcolm couldn't feel anything, but as the massage continued,he could actually feel a tingling sensation. It meant that Reese's method was really effective.He didn't say anything and just let her slowly massage him. The little girl seemed to be quite skilled,just by looking at her technique, he could tell she was an expert.

Reese wondered why Malcolm didn't say anything.Just as she was about to ask him, she noticed that he had turned his face to the side, lost in thought. The light from above cast different shadows on his face,and the area under his eyes was shaded by his eyelashes. His iris was a lighter color, showing a hint of gentleness.

This person was actually pretty good. He always defended her openly or secretly. It was just that she didn't want to put all her hopes on one man. If he really changed in the future and treated her poorly,she would be the only one hurt. As she thought of this, the light in Reese's eyes dimmed, and her movements weakened noticeably. Malcolm was already sure that she was slowly gaining consciousness.
