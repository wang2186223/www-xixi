Alyssa snarking while holding a cup of coffee.

Reese's confidence was through the roof, her aura even overshadowing Alyssa's. Alyssa was usually the queen bee at home,but for some reason, she felt a step down in front of Reese.

"Have you even looked at your ugly face in the mirror? Do you know what kind of people will be there? Socialites,all of them way more beautiful than you. The Flynn family is one of the top dogs in Atlanta. Don't drag them down. If you show up with Malcolm tonight, do you know what the headlines will be tomorrow?" She waved her hand dramatically, acting it out. "It'll definitely be Flynn family's Mr. Flynn's wife is hideous, lowering the family's standards."

No matter how much Alyssa tried to rile her up. Reese stayed cool and collected.

"If the Flynn family judges me by my looks, wouldn't that also lower your standards? No, it should be you lowering the Flynn family's quality."

Alyssa's face turned beet red, her shoulders shaking with rage.

She pointed at Reese furiously. "You..."

"T'll go if I want to. It's none of your business. Not even Malcolm can stop me."

Not only the Flynn family had the invite. Even without Malcolm,she could still get in. Alyssa couldn't get through to her. Reese was sharp-tongued.no matter what Alyssa said, Reese had a comeback ready.easily shutting her down.

When she looked into Reese's eyes earlier, she realized Reese wasn't like other girls. Her brows carried an unshakable arrogance,full of confidence.

Damn it, where did she get the nerve to think she could attend such a high-class event with that ugly face?

Since she couldn't convince her, it didn't matter. She'd just wait for Reese to embarrass the Flynn family and get thrown out by Grandpa. That was what she really wanted to see.
